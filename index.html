The Writer

Hi, I'm Alex Rivers.

I left a corporate job in 2018 with nothing but a notebook and a one-way ticket. What started as a personal travel diary became a blog read by over a million people — honest dispatches from 70+ countries, written for anyone who has ever wanted to go somewhere but didn't know how to begin.

I don't write tourist guides. I write about the in-between moments — the ones you never planned for, and never forget.
